Step 2: CCleaner Login

Step 3: Activating CCleaner Pro License Key

After successful login, follow these steps to activate CCleaner Pro:

  • Enter your valid CCleaner Pro license key in the input field.
  • Click Activate to complete the process.

If the license key is valid, you will see a confirmation message, and all Pro features will be unlocked immediately.
